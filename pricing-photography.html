Professional Photography

Photography Packages

Every frame is a considered choice. I photograph people, products, and places with patience, natural light, and film-inspired instinct.

What's Included

The full picture, from first light to final gallery

Photography is not just showing up with a camera. It is preparation, presence, patience, and craft — all of which come with every session.

01

The Camera

Fujifilm Mirrorless System

The Fujifilm X-H2S is a mirrorless camera built around exceptional color science and film simulation modes. Every image has a quality of light that sets it apart from conventional digital photography.

  • Fujifilm X-H2S with professional lens selection
  • Vintage glass available for organic character
  • RAW + JPEG capture for maximum flexibility
  • Macro and close-up detail lens work
02

The Light

Natural Light & Color

I prefer natural light because it is honest. When needed, I bring portable flash and diffusers — but always with the goal of light that looks found, not forced. Every gallery is color-processed with a consistent, film-inspired aesthetic.

  • Natural light preferred, portable flash available
  • Film-inspired color processing included
  • Consistent visual style across the full gallery
  • Skin-tone correction and exposure refinement
03

The Gallery

Delivery & Licensing

Your images are delivered through a private online gallery — fully edited, color-graded, and ready for print or screen. Clear licensing means you always know exactly how you can use your photographs.

  • 30+ final edited images (Portraiture)
  • High-resolution files, ready for print
  • Online gallery delivery within 5–7 business days
  • Commercial license included with Product/Brand

Creative Add-Ons

Deepen the Craft

Analogue character, intimate detail, classic glass — each add-on is a considered creative choice.

Vintage Glass

Adapted lenses from the 1960s–80s are imbued with organic bokeh, legendary flares, and gentle warmth modern optics can't replicate.

  • SMC Takumar 55mm
  • Юпитер (Jupiter) 9
  • Vivitar Series 1

35mm Film

When working with analogue film, every frame is deliberate before the shutter fires. The quality of film is very similar to digital, and each film recipe is strikingly artistic.

  • Kodak Portra 400
  • Kodak Gold 200
  • Ilford HP5
  • Fuji Superia 400

Macro Photography

Life-size or greater magnification reveals a world invisible to the naked eye. Ideal for products, textiles, botanicals, and jewellery — where detail is everything.

  • True macro 1:1 magnification
  • Jewellery, food, fabric, and botanical detail
  • Dedicated close-up lens setup
  • Studio lighting for precise detail

Lighting Package

Professional studio-grade lighting for controlled, commercial-quality results. Transforms any environment into a polished set — ideal for portraits, products, and brand shoots.

  • Aputure 600D key light
  • Softbox + reflector kit
  • Practical accent lights
  • Full setup + breakdown
