Custom projects can include any combination of creative add-ons. Mix and match to serve the story.

Vintage Glass

Adapted lenses from the 1960s–80s are imbued with organic bokeh, legendary flares, and gentle warmth modern optics can't replicate.

  • SMC Takumar 55mm
  • Юпитер (Jupiter) 9
  • Vivitar Series 1

35mm Film

When working with analogue film, every frame is deliberate before the shutter fires. Each film recipe is strikingly artistic.

  • Kodak Portra 400
  • Kodak Gold 200
  • Ilford HP5
  • Fuji Superia 400

Macro Photography

Life-size magnification for products, textiles, botanicals, and jewellery — where detail is everything.

  • True 1:1 macro magnification
  • Studio lighting setup
  • Jewellery, fabric, food detail

Drone Footage

Aerial perspective adds scale and cinematic sweep. Especially powerful for multi-day brand campaigns with outdoor locations.

  • Licensed drone operator
  • 4K aerial footage
  • Edited into final cut
  • Location-permitting

Full Audio Package

Multi-track sync recording, professional mastering, and music licensing. Real ambient sound captured on location.

  • Multi-track sync recording
  • Boom mic + lapel setup
  • Professional mastering
  • Music licensing included

Lighting Package

Professional continuous and strobe lighting for controlled, cinematic illumination across both video and photo deliverables in your campaign.

  • Aputure 600D key light

  • Practical accent lights
  • Full setup + breakdown

Long-Form Video

Full continuous recording with minimal editing — ideal for panels, keynotes, speeches, and events that need complete documentation alongside your highlight reel.

  • Full event recording
