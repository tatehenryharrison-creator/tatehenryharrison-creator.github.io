About Me

I'm Tate Harrison — a Boulder-based photographer and videographer with a background in audio production, narrative film, and human psychology. I believe the best creative work comes from understanding people and the stories they bring.

I want to launch your story into the online world through the most effective form of artistic communication: video and sound.

Tate has a B.A. in Psychology, and is a former photography instructor at Boulder Valley School District. Tate is training for his FAA Part 107 Drone Pilot License.

The Analog Touch

Vintage Glass & Film

Some stories deserve a softer edge. I shoot with adapted vintage lenses and 35mm film for clients who want something that feels less like content — and more like a document.

Vintage Glass
