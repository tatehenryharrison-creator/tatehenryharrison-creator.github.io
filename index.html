About

The Person Behind the Lens

I'm Tate Harrison — a Boulder-based photographer and videographer with a background in audio production, narrative film, and human psychology. I believe the best creative work comes from understanding people, not just equipment.

Whether I'm capturing a keynote on a main stage or following a subject through their daily life, my job is to make the frame feel honest. I started with an iPhone. I've since invested in the skills and tools to do the work justice.
