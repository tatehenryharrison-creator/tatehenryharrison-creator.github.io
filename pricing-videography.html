The Sound

Location Audio

My competitors use stock audio over their videos. I record real ambient sound on location and turn it into a cinematic soundtrack. Audio is not an afterthought — it is half the story.

  • Wireless lapel mic for primary subject
  • Ambient audio capture throughout the shoot
  • Professional audio mix and noise cleanup
  • Optional full audio package add-on available

The Grade

Color & Light

Color grading is the difference between footage and film. Every project receives a carefully considered grade that serves the mood and story — not just the exposure.

  • Natural light preferred, portable fill available
  • Film-inspired professional color grade included
  • Custom LUT for visual consistency across the project
  • Skin-tone calibration and exposure correction

Creative Add-Ons

Expand Your Production

Each add-on is a considered creative choice, not an upsell. Add what genuinely serves the story.

Vintage Glass

Adapted lenses from the 1960s–80s are imbued with organic bokeh, legendary flares, and gentle warmth modern optics can't replicate. Some stories deserve a softer edge.

  • SMC Takumar 55mm
  • Юпитер (Jupiter) 9
  • Vivitar Series 1

35mm Film

When working with analogue film, every frame is deliberate before the shutter fires. The quality of film is very similar to digital, and each film recipe is strikingly artistic.

  • Kodak Portra 400
  • Kodak Gold 200
  • Ilford HP5
  • Fuji Superia 400

Drone Footage

Aerial perspective adds scale, context, and cinematic sweep to any production. Drone footage transforms a good film into something genuinely impressive.

  • Licensed drone operator
  • 4K aerial footage
  • Fully edited into final cut
  • Location-permitting

Full Audio Package

Upgrade to a full audio production package with multi-track sync recording, professional mastering, and music licensing. Don't settle for stock songs online.

  • Multi-track sync recording
  • Boom mic + lapel setup
  • Professional audio mastering
  • Music licensing included

Lighting Package

Professional continuous and strobe lighting setup for controlled, cinematic illumination. Takes your production from documentary to high-end commercial quality.

  • Aputure 600D key light
  • Softbox + reflector kit
  • Practical accent lights
  • Full setup + breakdown

Long-Form Video

Full continuous recording with minimal editing — ideal for panels, keynotes, speeches, ceremonies, and events that need complete documentation rather than a cinematic highlight reel.

  • Full event recording
  • Minimal cuts / light edit
  • Ideal for speeches & panels
  • Delivered as single file

B-Roll Package

A dedicated session of supplemental footage — detail shots, environment cuts, process sequences — that gives the editor more to work with and the final film more texture and rhythm.

  • Dedicated B-roll session
  • Environment & detail shots
  • Woven into final edit
  • Adds depth and pacing

Macro / Close-Up Shots

Extreme close-up footage that reveals the texture and detail of your subject — perfect for products, food, jewellery, botanicals, or any moment where the tiny things tell the story.

  • True macro 1:1 footage
  • Texture and detail shots
  • Edited into final cut
  • Ideal for products & food
