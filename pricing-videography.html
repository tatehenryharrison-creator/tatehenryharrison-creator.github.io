The Camera

Cinema-Grade Video

I shoot on the Fujifilm X-H2S — a mirrorless system renowned for its exceptional color science and film simulation modes. The result is footage that feels intentional, warm, and alive.

  • Fujifilm X-H2S with cinema-calibrated color profiles
  • Single or multi-camera setup depending on package
  • B-roll, cutaway, and detail coverage included
  • Stabilized footage for smooth, professional results
02

The Sound

Location Audio

My competitors use stock audio over their videos. I record real ambient sound on location and turn it into a cinematic soundtrack. Audio is not an afterthought — it is half the story.

  • Wireless lapel mic for primary subject
  • Ambient audio capture throughout the shoot
  • Professional audio mix and noise cleanup
  • Optional full audio package add-on available
03

The Grade

Color & Light

Color grading is the difference between footage and film. Every project receives a carefully considered grade that serves the mood and story — not just the exposure.

  • Natural light preferred, portable fill available
  • Film-inspired professional color grade included
  • Custom LUT for visual consistency across the project
  • Skin-tone calibration and exposure correction
04

The Finish

Delivery & Rights

Clean file delivery, clear licensing, and revision rounds that respect your timeline. You get the final product ready for every screen — from Instagram to broadcast.

  • Edited final cut delivered in 5–10 business days
  • Social media cuts formatted for each platform
  • Revisions included (number varies by package)
  • Full commercial license on Novel and Epic packages

Creative Add-Ons

Expand Your Production

Each add-on is a considered creative choice, not an upsell. Add what genuinely serves the story.

Vintage Glass

Adapted lenses from the 1960s–80s are imbued with organic bokeh, legendary flares, and gentle warmth modern optics can't replicate. Some stories deserve a softer edge.

  • SMC Takumar 55mm
  • Юпитер (Jupiter) 9
  • Vivitar Series 1

35mm Film

When working with analogue film, every frame is deliberate before the shutter fires. The quality of film is very similar to digital, and each film recipe is strikingly artistic.

  • Kodak Portra 400
  • Kodak Gold 200
  • Ilford HP5
  • Fuji Superia 400

Drone Footage

Aerial perspective adds scale, context, and cinematic sweep to any production. Drone footage transforms a good film into something genuinely impressive.

  • Licensed drone operator
  • 4K aerial footage
  • Fully edited into final cut
  • Location-permitting

Full Audio Package

  • Multi-track sync recording
  • Boom mic + lapel setup
  • Professional audio mastering
  • Music licensing included
